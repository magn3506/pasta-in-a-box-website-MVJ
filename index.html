About

The company and restaurant PASTA IN A BOX started in 2017. Doing the location replacement of the popular street foot market in Copenhagen, the owners Maguns Jensen took the opportunity to make his visions and dreams come true. In 2018 he opened the first Pasta in a box store at reffen street food market. Pasta is common food but making freshly and handcrafted pasta is a task only a master can take on. It takes years of traning. Maguns Jensen has lived in Italy for 10 years doing an internship a Pasta de Italia in Rome. Here he learned how to make great pasta from the master of the craft. Massimo Milano. Maguns Jensen had a dream of making the delicious homemade pasta and making it available for all people in Denmark. It’s all about pasta. The concept is as simple as the name imply. IT’s ”PASTA IN A BOX” . Therefore the design is as simple as the concept. Putting freshly made pasta in a box for everyone to joy is a modern concept. Therefore the design of this brand is based in the same principles. With the simple and modern art style Flat design the brand has been able to successfully make their mark on the food industry.
